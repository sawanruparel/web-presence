## How to Use These

As you read, focus on three recurring questions:

1. What assumptions are hidden in the prototype?
2. Which constraints are being ignored because they are operational, not algorithmic?
3. Where will this design break first when real users and real environments are involved?

If this framing matches your own work, continue with **The Systems Playbook** for deeper frameworks.
