# DecisionRecord.io

A comprehensive guide to decision-making frameworks for engineering teams and organizations, co-authored with industry experts.

## Overview

DecisionRecord.io explores the critical role of decision-making in engineering organizations and provides practical frameworks for teams to make better, more consistent decisions. The book covers everything from individual decision-making processes to organizational decision architecture.

## Key Topics

- **Decision Frameworks**: Structured approaches to complex technical decisions
- **Team Dynamics**: How to facilitate effective group decision-making
- **Documentation**: Best practices for recording and communicating decisions
- **Organizational Learning**: Building decision-making capabilities across teams
- **Case Studies**: Real-world examples from successful engineering organizations

## Impact

The book has been adopted by engineering teams at companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500 organizations, helping them establish more effective decision-making processes and improve their technical outcomes.

## Availability

The book is available through major online retailers and can be found at [decisionrecord.io](https://decisionrecord.io).

## Co-authors

This work represents a collaboration with leading practitioners in engineering management and organizational design, bringing together diverse perspectives on effective decision-making in technical contexts.
